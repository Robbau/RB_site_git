With my background in human-computer interaction and industrial design, I involve methods of user-centered design as basis for a sustainable and value-driven tech development.

I believe design should enrich our day to day through culture, co-creation and community.

In my work at CASAVI I aim to involve the users voice in all design decision to evolve the product towards user value.
