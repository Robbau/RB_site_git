Robin Baudisch is a munich based designer with interests in product, interior and graphic design. Currently he is working for bulthaup kitchens as part of the in-house design team.

While his design studies at the University of Applied Science Munich he gained skills in digital and analoge product development as well as product management. His work is characterized by a deep understanding for the conceptional background of a product as well as an detailed interest in material and manufacturing processes.
