Stadthirsch Bicycles - 2016

In the beginning of the 20th century bikes where completely produced and molded by hand. Mostly in Italy and England this art of producing a bicycle on the user based body sizes was invented and further developed over the years. But with the price drop of automated production it faced a sudden death. Stadthirsch Bicycles resumes to this classic technic and is the attempt to build a balanced urban commuter bike on the base of a customizable concept. The Munich model is the first bike of the Stadthirsch series and embodies a speedy urban bike.
