Werktisch - bachelor thesis - 2017

In a more and more flexible working environment the understanding of a classic office changes radical. Its no longer just a tool but a connection point between family and job, leisure and work. The home office embodies this evolution. Located in the private space the personal claim and needs of the worker become more important. Though new kinds of interior concepts are needed. With Werktisch there is a customizable solution for these private offices, that offers storage and support for a variety of work types, and can change, grow and support all your personal needs.
